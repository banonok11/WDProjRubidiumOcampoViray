Beatdown

A beatdown deck is a type of deck archetype that specializes in offense. The main strategy that players of this type of deck use in matches are building up massive pushes (Placing high-elixir costing win conditions or normal cards) in order to overwhelm their opponent. Some examples of this type of deck are Golem Beatdown, Goblin Giant Beatdown, Giant Sparky Beatdown, etc.

This deck may sound powerful to a lot of players just by reading the description. However, it is important to note that you must still play smartly. This is because your pushes can be countered easily by lower elixir defense if your only strategy is to spam massive pushes.
