Siege

Siege is a very special type of deck archetype in Clash Royale. While other decks use troops that cross the bridge in order to damage the tower, siege decks rely on offensive buildings that are able to reach the opponentâ€™s tower and attack from a distance. These decks require strong defense as siege buildings need protection and time in order to inflict more damage.

This deck archetype is very versatile but is considered one of the more harder type of decks to be used. You need a high understanding of the game in order to play this archetype. The most commonly used siege buildings are X-Bow and Mortar.
