Control

A control deck is a type of deck archetype that specializes in defense. These type of decks are to be played reactively and slow-paced. They are able to easily defend with the cards they have (Ex: Knight, Ice Wizard, Goblin Hut, Minions, Goblin Gang, Miner, etc.) and are able to initiate a counter push with their win condition if the opportunity for one is open. Users of this deck have the ability to control the pace of the game to their liking. Some famous players that use control decks well are Ian77, Surgical Goblin and Riley. Overall, this archetype is very versatile and is guaranteed to be strong if paired up with the right user.
