Bridgespam

A bridgespam deck is exactly what its name hints at, spamming troops at the bridge. Similar to beatdown decks, users are also able to overwhelm their opponents with massive pushes. However, even if the name says “bridge spam”, take note that you must first get an elixir lead or make sure that your opponent does not have any counters to your pushes (Other strategies are also applicable).

Similar to beatdown decks, users are also able to overwhelm their opponents with massive pushes. The key difference is that bridge spam decks use rush attacks which is another term for placing them behind the bridge. Some units that can be used for this strategy are Bandit, Battle Ram, Dark Prince, etc.
