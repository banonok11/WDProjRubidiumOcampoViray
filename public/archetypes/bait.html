Bait

A bait deck is a type of deck archetype that specializes in bringing out an opponent’s counter spell and punishing them with a counter push. Bait decks mainly rely on “chip damage” which is relying on small chunks of damage on the tower in order to take it down. Some examples of bait decks are log bait, fireball bait, and hyper bait.

To put it simply, it is a deck that is able to easily defeat your opponent if played smartly. This deck is very recognized in the Clash Royale community as it is one of the most used out there by famous players such as Riley and Mohammed Light.
