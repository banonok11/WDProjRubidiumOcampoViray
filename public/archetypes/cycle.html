Cycle

A cycle deck is a type of deck archetype that specializes in defense and fast cycles as these decks require cheap cards. It is a very effective deck as you are able to punish your opponent by cycling faster to your win condition if they have a more expensive deck. This deck archetype is able to throw off your opponent as their cycle would get messed up and they would be lacking in defense.

The main strategy in this archetype is to 'out-cycle' your opponent. This simply means that you have to cycle faster than your opponent can cycle to the counters for your cards. Some famous cycle decks are Hog 2.6, Hog EQ, Piggies EQ. These decks are mainly in the range of 2.6 (can be lower) - 3.0 average elixir.
