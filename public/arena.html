Arena Progression Guide

Learn what to expect in each arena. Arena progression is your core experience in Clash Royale. Each arena introduces new cards and strategies that change how games are played.

This guide provides an overview of each arena, including trophy ranges, cards to expect, and tips for progressing through the arenas effectively. You will learn how strategies change and how to adapt your deck along the way. A video is also included for a more detailed guide to help you in your journey.
